About Us

Investment is an old business, but there is a new buzzword in town – “Cryptocurrencies”. Suddenly, everyone wants to invest in this new market. Although, there are plethora of crypto currency exchange platforms operating from various parts of the globe, each of these platforms offer different exchange rates and incentives to the user. This makes investment in cryptos a cumbersome effort despite of its popularity.


At CoinPanda, we try to mend this gap. We aim to make investment in cryptocurrencies an easy and a smooth affair for our users. We bring details and data from all the exchange platforms together for our users on a single screen in a simplified manner. Our users can leverage our unique feature of crypto platforms comparisons which will facilitate and ease their investment decisions. Moreover, for naive users it is the perfect place to learn about cryptocurrencies and understand trends behind the blockchain technology.
